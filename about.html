About Us



Drawing inspiration from the mysteries of space, Galaxica Playland is a unique amusement park that provides guests with an exciting and immersive experience. For visitors of all ages, the park offers thrilling rides, interactive activities, and space-themed excursions. There is plenty for everyone, including entertaining educational exhibitions about far-off galaxies and space-travel simulation roller coasters.

The park was established to give thrill-seekers and families an enjoyable, secure, and unforgettable experience. With an emphasis on exceptional customer care and constantly changing attractions, Galaxica Playland guarantees an incredible experience that will entice visitors to return time and time again
